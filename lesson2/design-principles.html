White Space

Google

https://www.google.com/

Google's website is a good example of White space and clean design in web development. When someone goes to Google's webpage, they are almost always going there for one reason only - to do a search. Google realized this and instead of making the user navigate through a bunch of adds the search bar is right in the middle of the page without anything around it. White space does not necessarily need to be white but in this case it is.

Hicks Law

Pepsi

https://www.pepsi.com/

Pepsi website is an example of Hicks Law. Hicks Law states that the more choices you present yours users with , the longer it will take them to reach a decision. pepsi only has a few options on its main website. It probably has many more linked subpages to it but, a user can navigate teh main site easily.

P.A.R.C. Alignment

Pizza hut

https://www.pizzahut.com/

Pizza hut has a lot of content and choices that a user will have to make on its website. The overall navigation and use of the website is easy however becasue it uses the design element of alignment.
